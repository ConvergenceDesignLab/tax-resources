1099 Form

At the end of the year, all independent contractors that complete a W-9 should receive a 1099 form with information about how much money you made from the organization, if you received more than $600. Depending upon how much you earned, you may need to file a tax return. You are required to file a tax return if you earn $400 or more as an independent contractor even if you do not receive a 1099. To get free help with your taxes during tax season, look up Vita Sites in your area. This service is only available during tax season (January - April).

Please be sure to keep a copy of your 1099 safe and accessible to complete your taxes and be sure to keep a record of your employer's contact information in case you have any questions.

Taxes must be filed by April 15th for most people.
