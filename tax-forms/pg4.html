Your taxes

At the end of the year, your employer will send you a W-2 form with information about how much money you made. You may need to file a tax return. To get free help with your taxes during tax season, look up Vita Sites in your area. This service is only available during tax season (January - April).

Please be sure to keep a copy of your W-2 to complete your taxes and be sure to keep a record of your employer's contact information in case you have any questions.
