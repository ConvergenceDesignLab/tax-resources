Where to obtain State Identification:

  • Birth Certificate: www.dph.illinois.gov
  • Social Security Card: www.ssa.gov
  • State ID: www.cyberdriveillinois.com
  • (If needed) Matricula Card: The matricula card is an ID for Mexican nationals. Contact your local consulate at www.embassyofmexico.org, and to find other consulates check www.consumer-action.org.
