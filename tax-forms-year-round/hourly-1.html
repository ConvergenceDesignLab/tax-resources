I-9 Forms: The First Step to Starting Work

The I-9 Form is a form required by the government to verify employment eligibility. You will need one or two documents to prove your eligibility, and then you will need to sign a page stating you are eligible for employment in the United States.

Which documents to bring:

  • A complete list of acceptable documents can be found in the I-9 form’s instructions, and by law, you are allowed to use any of the documents listed, but almost everyone uses:
    • Just their US passport or foreign passport containing their visa or permanent resident card.
  • Or, one of EACH of the following:
    • Driver’s License or State ID or School ID
    • U.S. Birth Certificate or Social Security Card

Click here to view an I-9 form.

You should bring these documents on your first day of work or to your orientation as instructed, BUT you must bring the ORIGINAL documents as copies are not acceptable. Be sure to keep your documents SAFE and to only show them to someone you trust like your employer or your banker/financial institution representative/college, etc.
